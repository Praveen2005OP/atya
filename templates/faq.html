Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Atya.

What is Atya?

Atya is an automotive research platform designed to help users discover, compare, and understand cars through detailed specifications, variant-wise breakdowns, feature analysis, safety information, and comparison tools.

Is Atya free to use?

Yes. Atya is currently available free of charge and aims to make automotive information more accessible to everyone.

How accurate is the information on Atya?

We strive to provide accurate and up-to-date information sourced from manufacturers and trusted references. However, specifications, prices, and features may change over time. Users should verify critical information with manufacturers or dealerships.

Can I compare different cars?

Yes. Atya provides comparison tools that allow users to evaluate vehicles side-by-side across specifications, features, performance, pricing, and safety information.

Does Atya sell cars?

No. Atya is an automotive information platform and does not directly sell, lease, or broker vehicles.

How often is vehicle data updated?

We regularly review and update vehicle information whenever manufacturers release new variants, feature updates, pricing revisions, or model-year changes.

Can I save cars for later?

Yes. Atya allows users to save vehicles and quickly access them for future research and comparisons.

Why are some features different between variants?

Manufacturers often offer different equipment, technology, safety features, and comfort options across variants. Atya highlights these differences to help users make informed decisions.

How can I report incorrect information?

If you find inaccurate or outdated information, please contact us through the channels provided on the website. We appreciate community feedback and continuously work to improve data quality.

How can I contact Atya?

You can reach us through our contact channels, social media profiles, or feedback forms available on the website.

Questions drive discovery. Atya exists to make automotive research simpler, clearer, and smarter.
